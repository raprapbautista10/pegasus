Patrick Whelan, a senior level financial executive with extensive transaction-related expertise, founded Pegasus in 1997. Mr. Whelan's career in the leveraged buyout, venture capital and investment banking industries spans more than twenty years.

Mr. Whelan leads Pegasus's investment activities and completes all of the firm's acquisitions. His contributions include identifying and analyzing target companies, negotiating deal structure and terms, coordinating due diligence, raising debt capital and supporting management teams post-transaction.

Prior to founding Pegasus, Mr. Whelan completed merger and acquisition transactions for Broadview International, one of the world's leading niche M&A investment banking firms. At Broadview, he held the position of Co-Managing Director. For the six previous years, Mr. Whelan held senior positions in two different private equity firms, Abacus Ventures of Greenwich, CT and InterFirst Ventures of Dallas, TX. During his tenure at these firms, he made numerous investments in rapidly growing companies. Mr. Whelan was a founding investor and served on the Board of Directors of TranSwitch Corporation (NASDAQ) and Teleos Communications (NASDAQ). He also served on the Board of Directors of TelWatch Corporation and Quality Components, Inc.

Mr. Whelan holds an MBA degree in Finance from The Wharton School and is an Honors graduate of Vanderbilt University with degrees in Economics and Computer Science. He is currently Chairman of Poma Corporation, TOG Manufacturing Company and Shield Pack, LLC. He is a Director of Calwax Corporation and Borga, Inc.
