Pegasus Management Group has a successful history of acquiring and growing middle market companies.  We specialize in buying established, profitable businesses with significant growth potential in non-technology intensive industries.  Our current portfolio consists of three high profit margin manufacturing companies located throughout the United States.
Overview

 

Introduction

 

Pegasus was founded in 1994 with the specific purpose of acquiring and growing, profitable middle-market companies located in the United States and Canada.  We purchase high quality companies with established track records and clear opportunities for continued growth.  Acquisition candidates include privately held corporations and divisions or subsidiaries of larger corporations.

 

Investment Philosophy

 

Pegasus invests across a wide range of industries with a focus on established, non-technology intensive niche manufacturing companies.  We also consider value-added distributors, direct marketers of consumer products and specialty service companies.

 

We target companies that exhibit strong, defensible market positions, clear growth opportunities and high profit margins.  Our outlook is long-term, with a focus on building the value of each acquired company over time.  We seek companies that are currently or have the potential to become leaders in their industry niche.

 

Except in rare cases, start-ups, early stage ventures, turnarounds, technology-driven companies and minority investments are not of interest.

 

Working With Management

 

The foundation of our approach is to partner with quality managers who have the experience and dedication to profitably grow their business.  Management, not Pegasus, is responsible for each portfolio company’s day-to-day operations.

 

Pegasus’s role is to work with management to develop growth opportunities for the company.  We help establish corporate goals, provide strategic advice and act as a sounding board for management.  Pegasus analyzes new markets, makes introductions to potential customers, identifies and evaluates acquisition opportunities, recruits executives, raises additional capital and provides access to our network of business and financial contacts.

 

We believe in establishing common goals with our management teams.  Pegasus provides equity incentives to portfolio company management to ensure that they are rewarded for the value they create.

Acquisition Criteria

 

Pegasus seeks controlling positions in quality companies with $5 to $50 million in annual revenue and a minimum cash flow of $1.5 million.  Acquisition candidates should demonstrate profits in each of the last two years and have strong growth prospects for the future, either internally or through additional acquisitions.

Management

 

Patrick F. Whelan

President

 

Patrick Whelan, a senior level financial executive with extensive transaction-related expertise, founded Pegasus in 1994.  Mr. Whelan’s career in the leveraged buyout, venture capital and investment banking industries spans more than seventeen years.

 

Mr. Whelan leads Pegasus’s investment activities and he is credited with completing all of the firm’s acquisitions.  His contributions include identifying and analyzing target companies, negotiating deal structure and terms, coordinating due diligence, raising debt capital and supporting management teams post-transaction.

 

Prior to founding Pegasus, Mr. Whelan completed merger and acquisition transactions for Broadview Associates, one of the world's leading niche M&A investment banking firms.  He held the position of Co-Manager, Hardware Equipment Practice.  For the six previous years, Mr. Whelan held senior positions in two different private equity firms, Abacus Ventures of Greenwich, CT and InterFirst Ventures of Dallas, TX.  During his tenure at these firms, he made numerous investments in rapidly growing companies.  Mr. Whelan was a founding investor and served on the Board of Directors of TranSwitch Corporation (NASDAQ) and Teleos Communications (NASDAQ).  Mr. Whelan also served on the Board of Directors of TelWatch Corporation and Quality Components, Inc.

 

Mr. Whelan holds an MBA degree in Finance from The Wharton School and is an Honors graduate of Vanderbilt University with degrees in Economics and Computer Science.  He is currently Chairman of both Poma Corporation and TOG Manufacturing Company and is a Director of Calwax Corporation.

Working With Intermediaries

 

The vast majority of our investment opportunities come from our network of investment bankers, business brokers, accountants, lenders, and attorneys.  If a fee is not already due from the seller, Pegasus will pay a competitive fee to anyone introducing us to an opportunity that leads to a completed acquisition.
