Pegasus seeks controlling positions in quality companies with $5 to $75 million in annual revenue and a minimum cash flow of $1.5 million. Acquisition candidates should demonstrate profits in each of the last two years and have strong growth prospects for the future, either internally or through additional acquisitions.

Attractive candidates possess a majority of the following characteristics:

Types of Businesses
• Privately held businesses or a subsidiary or division of a public corporation

Industries
• Non-technology driven
• Preference for niche manufacturing companies
• Also consider value-added distributors and specialty service companies

Size
• EBITDA of $1.5 to $7.5 million, after adjustment for owner-related expenses
• Revenue of $5 to $75 million
• No minimum size for add-on acquisitions

Geography
• Located throughout North America

Profitability
• Profitable during the two most recent years
• Sustainable EBITDA margins of 12% or greater

Growth Potential
• Significant growth opportunities either through internal expansion or external acquisition
• Untapped growth potential due to a lack of capital and/or management desire

Management Continuity
• Management continuity is not required

Other
• Non-cyclical market
• Significant barriers to entry
• Diversified, stable customer base

Pegasus generally does not pursue start-ups, early stage ventures, minority investments, or high technology companies.
