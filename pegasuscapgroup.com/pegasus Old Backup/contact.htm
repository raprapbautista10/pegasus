Working With Intermediaries
The vast majority of our investment opportunities come from our network of investment bankers, business brokers, accountants, lenders and attorneys. If a fee is not already due from the selling company, Pegasus will pay a competitive fee to anyone introducing us to an opportunity that leads to a completed acquisition.


Management Employment Opportunities
Pegasus is often required to hire a new President and/or fill other senior management positions for our portfolio companies. We encourage contact both by executives interested in filling such positions as well as from other managers seeking capital to complete their own targeted acquisitions.
