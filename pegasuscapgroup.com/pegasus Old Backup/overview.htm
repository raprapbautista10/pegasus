Introduction
Pegasus was founded in 1997 with the specific purpose of acquiring and growing profitable middle-market companies. We purchase high quality companies with established track records and clear opportunities for growth. Acquisition candidates include privately held corporations and divisions or subsidiaries of larger corporations.

Investment Philosophy
Pegasus invests across a wide range of industries with a focus on established, non-technology intensive niche manufacturing companies. We also consider value-added distributors and specialty service companies.

We target companies that exhibit strong, defensible market positions, distinct growth opportunities and high profit margins. Our outlook is long-term, with a focus on building the value of each acquired company over time. We seek companies that are currently or have the potential to become leaders in their industry niche.

Except in rare cases, start-ups, early stage ventures, technology-driven companies and minority investments are not of interest.

Working With Management
The foundation of our approach is to partner with quality managers who have the experience and dedication to profitably grow their business. Management, not Pegasus, is responsible for each portfolio company's day-to-day operations.

Pegasus's role is to work with management to develop growth opportunities for each company. We help establish corporate goals, provide strategic advice and act as a sounding board for management. Pegasus analyzes new markets, makes introductions to potential customers, identifies and evaluates acquisition opportunities, recruits executives, raises additional capital and provides access to our network of business and financial contacts.

We believe in establishing common goals with our management teams. Pegasus provides equity incentives to portfolio company management to ensure that they are rewarded for the value they create.
